Our Mission

Tie Dye for Autism is a Non-Profit organization that aims to provide help and an education to underpriveleged children with Autism Spectrum Disorder.

All of the profits earned from selling homemade tie-dye shirts are funnelled to schools in India that help those that are affected.

Where are the events held?

The events are currently hosted in the tri-state area.

What do I bring to an event?

All supplies are provided. Just bring yourself and clothes that you are not afraid to get stained!

I want to volunteer but I am not located in the tri-state area. How do I take action?

We are very grateful for your interest in supporting the affected. However, for the time being, we only host events in a limited region. Please consider contributing to our organization through donations or purchasing the shirts!

How can I donate to Tie Dye for Autism?

Upon entering the website, you will be greeted with many opportunities to donate. Simply click on one of the buttons when prompted and you will be directed to the proper site. You are one step closer to helping disadvantaged children in India!

Where is my donation going?

Your generous donation will be hand-delivered to the caretakers of affected children by our staff.
